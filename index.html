      My Rewards

      How do Hays Rewards work?

      The more you use the card, the greater your rewards! Your card earns you rewards each and every time you shop, and it’s FREE! You choose where you shop for groceries – why not shop where you can save even more?

       

      Hays Rewards do not affect prices!

      You’ll find the same great low prices every day, with or without the card.

       

      How do I earn rewards?

      You earn rewards points for every dollar you spend. Points add up automatically after each shopping trip. Check the bottom of your receipt for your current rewards balance.

      So, tell me about those rewards...

      Earn Points and Save 5% on Groceries

      Customers can earn five points for every dollar spent on participating items. Once a customer accumulates 2,500 points within a 90 day period, the customer will receive an offer to get 5% off a future transaction of up to $100. Click to see how it works.

       

      Learn More

      Hays Wine Rewards

      Each time a customer purchases six bottles of wine, the customer will receive a reward of 20% off the retail price of the next bottle they purchase.

       

      Learn More

      Hays Salad Bar Rewards

      Each time a customer purchases six salads or soups from our salad bar, the customer will receive a reward of 50% off the retail price of the next salad or soup they purchase from our salad bar.
